[SPEAKERSPRITE=NPC Guide][NAME=NPC Guide]Welcome to this dungeon!
You can use arrow keys to move and can fight the monsters by using your mouse.
Be careful though, make sure not to touch the monsters or you'll lose a heart!
In this dungeon you will encounter green platforms, they must be activated by pushing the potted plants on them.
If you encounter lava in the dungeon be careful and make sure not to touch it! 
You'll have to go through a series of different challenges to make it through this dungeon!
Good luck!
